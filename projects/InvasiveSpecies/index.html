Species that are directly affected by the presence of this species


Homo Sapiens affect animals like moose, deer, fish, etc by hunting them down illegally for food and for joy and this causes a decrease in population for the animal. Homo Sapiens also kill plants and trees by deforestation.

How does this benefit/harm homo sapiens


Hunting down animals or fishing benefits us Homo Sapiens because we get more food supply. More seafood and meat will pop up in markets and people can buy it for food. Fishing harms us humans because in a few years, if we overfish we will start to run out of seafood. The same could be said about hunting, if we over hunt we start to run out of meat in a few years Deforestation benefits homo sapiens because the trees from the forest that they cut down are used for construction and building materials for building houses. Deforestation doesn’t only provide shelter for homo sapiens but also to boon construction industry. Deforestation also harms humans because there will be less oxygen in the air because of the trees being cut down.

Human endeavors to correct the imbalance- how are they working


In order to help correct the imbalance of deforestation, homo sapien organizations are working towards making it illegal. “Amazon Watch works at protecting indigenous communities living in the Amazon Rainforest, while others like the Sierra Club and the World Wildlife Fund seek to protect forested areas around the world.” “The Environmental Defense Fund push for bills that give landowners financial incentives to practice land conservation.” replanting trees for those lost is another solution to helping correct the imbalance of deforestation. China is an example, in China each citizen must plant three trees per year. Because of this In 2009 5.9 million hectares of forest got added. The United States managed a net gain in forest cover as a result of reforestation. Other countries are still against reforestation. In order to correct the imbalance of hunting (and fishing) there are laws as well as regulations strictly enforced regarding where you can hunt, when, what animals, how many of them, etc. You could say the same for fishing.
